Particle Generator - Physics Based

Architected a particle generator that generates tens of thousands of particles each deriving its attribute from a random distribution with an option to export the simulation as an .obj file.

Used Stack data structure to efficiently store/discard particles thereby reusing memory locations of dead particles.

Wrote a custom Python Surface Operator (SOP) in Houdini that reads the simulation from the obj file and visualize the simulation using Mantra Renderer

Flocking Simulation - Harry Potter VR

The simulation has Two flock systems, each having its own team leader and different flock behaviours. The player will be the leader of one team and is able to switch through three different flock formations (members flock around team leader, members stay ahead of team leader, members stay behind team leader)

The Wand which is on the right controller emits particles in burst mode and the particles gets destroyed after they die out.

The Flocking system, particle generator is custom based and does not use Unity AI/ Unity particle system.

Crowd Simulation - Animal Behavior

Currently working on simulating the behavior of wolves and bison when they confront each other

Wolves and Bisons both exhibit the behavior of following the animal in front of them (foraging ant behavior)

Custom Solver has to be written in Houdini to simulate unique behavior shown by both crowds

The aim of this project is to simulate the changes in the crowds behavior due to external factors (predator, prey, etc)
